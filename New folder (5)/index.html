About Us

Professional Photographer & Calligraphy Artist

This is owned by Filipino photographer BJ Pascual, one of Manila’s biggest names. He’s the city’s go-to celebrity fashion photographer whose work has featured on countless magazine covers (including this one), high profile advertising campaigns and popular pan-continental TV show, Asia’s Next Top Model. He is also a Calligraphy Artist, that creates an artwork to express himself.
